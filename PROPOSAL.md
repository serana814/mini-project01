## Client Brief

**Client:** The client is Camila Eraña. My younger sister who shares a granola business in our hometown with one of our cousins. Her Business is called CamiLu Treats. It is a delicious, healthy, homemade granola recipe that they have been perfecting over the last few years. 

**Purpose:** The purpose of this website is to create awareness about her business and display the catalog of products that she has created so that clients can place orders. 

**Audience:** Adults and children that are in our hometown community. They will need to see a colorful website that will promote their products and have the same loving energy that they put into their treats. 

**Key action:** Browsers should scroll through the catalog of treats and use the contact information to place orders of the treats they would like. 

**Pages/sections needed:**
- Home page
- About page
- Products Page
- Contact Page
- Navigation Bar
- Testimonials/Reviews Page

**Content status:** I have some images such as the logo and basic information on the company. I would still need the client to send me images of the final product in order to display for customers. I also need the client to send me descriptions of each of the products and a brief description or message from the company to add to the main page. I also requested images of the process because I feel like it would be nice and apprpriate to create a gallery for things such as the baking process, happy customers, and previous sales points. 

**Style preferences:** - Primary Color: #DBB8B0, Secondary Color: #1A223F, Background Color: #F7EFEA Font 1: Brittany Signature, Font 2: Monserrat. They requested a very light and girly feel to the website to showcase that they are young girls who are creating the granola. They want it to feel fun and fresh.
